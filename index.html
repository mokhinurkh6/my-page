About me

Hi,

My name is Sherzod and I am a Master's student at the University of South Florida and a Software Engineer Intern at John Deere.

I am also a Co-Founder of the Turkic Interlingua (TIL), a research community dedicated to developing language technologies for low-resource languages.

Duv-Duv Gap (NLP framework)

A language agnostic framework for predicting news engagement rate in an unbiased manner. The winner of NewsQ for Social Good Challenge at HackGT7 Hackathon.

https://devpost.com/software/duv-duv-gap

VideoSurfer (Flask Web App)

Using VideoSurfer, users will be able to either a) upload a video of their own or b) provide a Youtube link. We preprocess the video and enable the following 3 features: a) search for scenes using keywords/sentences spoken in the video b) search for scenes by using real life human descriptions (i.e. Joey drinking milk in the kitchen) and c) search for Ebay products using descriptions/keywords.

https://devpost.com/software/videosurfer

Fuzzy Patients (Challenge)

This project solves the Hacktech 2020 Office Ally challenge 1: Refining the Patient Matching Algorithm. We present a web dashboard where you can input your own data and use our different algorithms to cluster same patients into clusters using Levenshtein distance.
